Rhea Ramakrishnan

Rhea Ramakrishnan is a writer based in Baltimore, MD.

Her poems and short fiction have appeared in a few different lit journals and zines as well as self-published in collaborative blogs such as Can I Text U and For Blown Speakers.

She graduated from the University of Maryland in 2015 after studying Biology and Creative Writing.

From 2015-2016 she lived in Cleveland and worked for an education-based non-profit.

She is interested in creating stuff with other people.
