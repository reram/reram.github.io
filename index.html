Rhea Ramakrishnan

I am a writer and maker based in Baltimore, MD. My poems and short fiction have appeared in a few different lit journals and zines as well as self-published in collaborative blogs such as Can I Text U and For Blown Speakers. I believe that we as humans are innately creative and that art and innovation should be accessible to everyone. I am always interested in creating stuff with other people.
